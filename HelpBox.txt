LENGTH
+ Usernames must be 5 - 60 characters long
+Passwords must be 12 - 24 characters long

SPECIAL CHARACTERS
+ Usernames can contain any combination of letters
  (Aa - Zz), numbers (0 - 9), or the following
  characters ( _ @ . )
+ Passwords must contain any combination of letters
  (Aa - Zz), numbers (0 - 9), and 2 or more ofthe
  following characters ( !@#$()%&_ )
